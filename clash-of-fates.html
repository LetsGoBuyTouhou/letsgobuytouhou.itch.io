Resume

An old client came back to me and said, "Hey remember one of the concept kits you did for Copper? Can you re-design a new character off of that because I absolutely loved it."

 

Acerona: The Enlightened One

 

A star that once guarded the gates to the underworld. She would make conversation with many monks and wise-men while they awaited judgment- alongside people and beings of all different types. It was from this point, where she understood what was truly important and she ventured to the living world, seeking to protect and prevent the living from deserving a bad judgment.

Passive

Void Shift-

If an ally nearby has died, Acerona enters Void Shift, gaining raised attack speed, AP, Attack, Defense, becomes melee, and new spells. Celestial release cannot be used, and will be interrupted. This effect remains until the ally is revived.

Q

Yama's Star-

Sends energy in a skill shot, silencing and dealing small damage to all champions hit.

W

Stardust Reconstruction-

A high-cooldown spell that heals target and removes debuffs

E

Illusionist-

 A Fast traveling skill shot, turns into a visual copy of the first one hit.

R

Celestial Release:

Range is increased, AP increased, speed increased 10%, +speed on spell casts.

Scales 5/8/10 seconds

Q

Void Siphon:

Deals damage to enemies around the target, as well as slows and deals massive damage to the target. Point and click.

W

Infinity Rift

Sends a slash of black energy, dealing tons of damage in an AOE. Skill shot, damaging all it hits.

E

Wormhole-

Opens a portal only Acerona can use to a target location, the portal will damage anyone who touches it.  2- charge Skillshot.
