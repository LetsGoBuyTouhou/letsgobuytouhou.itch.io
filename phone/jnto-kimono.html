JNTO: Kimono Extinction

Photography Priscilla McGann

Kimono Modern Antenna, Globus Washitsu Gallery

Problem Kimono makers are in fear of becoming extinct because of their low demand, so the JNTO wants to encourage people to learn about Japan's culture and consider the potential the Kimono has.

 

Insight Two staples of Japanese fashion- Uniqlo and Muji with two respected US retailers- Target and Macys have the potential to pioneer this event.
