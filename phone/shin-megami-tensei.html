Experience

First, guests are given RFC Nicaea Business Cards that correlate to one of the main characters of the game from Tico, a Nicaea worker.

 

(Yes, she's from Persona, but it's the closest I could get to Tico.)

Inside, the character assigned to the card will replace the holder's reflection in a house of mirrors.

 

Simple cameras and imaging technology will ensure the models follow the holders' movement, where users can play around and have fun.

 

At a certain point, a cutscene will play on one wall, prompting users to agree to Nicaea's Services for their reflections to be saved from an oncoming train.

If you agree to you pre-order the game at the end, your Nicaea Business Card can be scanned at your game store to receive your game, and your card is stamped with "Devil Survivor"
