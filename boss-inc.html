Passive

Chihuahua Magic-

If an ally dies, they will be able to command Otto, with increased damage, for a short duration. Passive has a cooldown.

Chihuahua Cannon-

Sparky fires a chihuahua out of a cannon. Point and click.

W

Summon: The Annoyance-

Sparky summons Otto for 15 seconds

Rally-

Sparky rallies the chihuahuas, gaining attack speed and armor for him and Otto. Nearby minions also want to become Chihuahuas so bad, they are rallied as well.

Heart of the Small Dog-

Sparky calls his Chihuahua Army to stampede forward, dealing damage and knocking up enemies hit. Skill shot.

Fighter/Tank

Passive

Sociality Core-

When low on health, the Sociality Core activates its emergency mode, increasing armor and magic resist.

 

 

Q

Sensory Corruption-

Sparkle creates a slightly curved wall in the terrain. This is impassible except by a blink or dash.

 

W

Ultimate Processor-

Sparkle gains 10% movement speed and a shield that scales off missing health.

E

Rage Encryption-

CD: When at full rage.

 

Passive: Sparkle gains rage for every time she takes or deals damage (including spell casts), rage decays slowly. When at full rage, Sparkle’s AD increases by a percentage of her resistances.

 

Active: Sparkle unleashes her rage, stunning an enemy with the next basic attack and dealing magic damage. Stun duration scales with target’s max health.

R

Fragment Memory-

Range: Global Skill shot.

Sparkle fires a line across the map, that after a short delay, activates at the same time at all points. This corrupts enemy memories, scrambling the numbers on their cooldowns and health bar. This is a skill shot but not a projectile.

 

Duration: 5/6.5/8

The duration decreases 1 second for each enemy hit, for a minimum of 2 seconds.
